1.Connect the controller with a serial USB to computer.

2.Open Arduino program then open Serial monitor (using shortcut "ctrl+shift+m")

3.Command the voltage needed by typing number 12-80, Command the relay by typing "on" or "off"

P.S. every time user assign new voltage needed, the relay will turn off automatically for safety purpose. 
please turn it on manually by type "on" into serial monitor.
